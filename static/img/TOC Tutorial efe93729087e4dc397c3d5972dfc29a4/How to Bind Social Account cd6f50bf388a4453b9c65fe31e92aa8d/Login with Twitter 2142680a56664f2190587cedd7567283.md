# Login with Twitter

Step 1 Enter the taskon.xyz website address in your browser.

![11.png](Login%20with%20Twitter%202142680a56664f2190587cedd7567283/11.png)

Step 2 Click on the "Login" button located in the upper right corner of the screen

![12.png](Login%20with%20Twitter%202142680a56664f2190587cedd7567283/12.png)

Step 3 Select Twitter from the drop-down menu that appears.

![13.png](Login%20with%20Twitter%202142680a56664f2190587cedd7567283/13.png)

Step 4 Click "Authorize app" to confirm the login process.

![Untitled](Login%20with%20Twitter%202142680a56664f2190587cedd7567283/Untitled.png)

Step 5 You can see your Twitter handle in the upper right corner.

![Untitled](Login%20with%20Twitter%202142680a56664f2190587cedd7567283/Untitled%201.png)

Step 6 Click on "Participant Center" in the dropdown menu.

![14.png](Login%20with%20Twitter%202142680a56664f2190587cedd7567283/14.png)

Click "Edit" on your personal page.

![15.png](Login%20with%20Twitter%202142680a56664f2190587cedd7567283/15.png)

You can view your Twitter login information. 

![16.png](Login%20with%20Twitter%202142680a56664f2190587cedd7567283/16.png)
